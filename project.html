I don't just build projects; I go on epic quests to solve problems that may or may not exist. I've done it all. My secret weapon? The internet community and god bless LLMs.

  • Adaptive Spectral Alignment Networks Built from scratch a deep learning architecture for time series alignment that adaptively learns spectral representations and alignment paths. The network combines spectral analysis with learned transformations to handle complex temporal patterns and phase shifts in multivariate time series data.
    Tech and Frameworks:PyTorch, Graph Neural Networks, Dynamic Time Warping, Spectral Analysis

  • Mixture of Experts (MoE) Architecture Implemented a Mixture of Experts model from scratch, including the routing mechanism, expert selection, and load balancing. The architecture efficiently scales model capacity by activating only a subset of expert networks for each input, enabling larger models with manageable computational costs.
    Tech and Frameworks:PyTorch, Distributed Training, CUDA, Sparse Activation

  • Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) Developed GRPO from scratch, a reinforcement learning algorithm that optimizes policies using group-relative comparisons. The implementation includes the core algorithm, reward shaping mechanisms, and training infrastructure for both discrete and continuous action spaces.
    Tech and Frameworks:PyTorch, Reinforcement Learning, Policy Gradients, CUDA
